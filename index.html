I go by the name of steenuil. You might recognize it as the dutch name of the Athene Noctua, also known as the little owl in english.

I write software every now and then, most of which could be classified as yak shaving around small issues I have with the programs I use every day.

I like revisiting old things using new tools, so I often end up spelunking through old, semi-abandoned codebases as research. Sometimes I even try to get them working again.

I currently dwell in Italy, not too far from the Alps. I speak English and Italian, and I know enough French to get by.
